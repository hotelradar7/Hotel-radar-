Our Story

Built to Serve India's Real Travellers

StayIndia was born from a simple frustration: when Indians searched for hotels in pilgrimage towns, hill stations, and smaller cities, they found outdated listings, fake photos, and platforms charging hotels 15–25% commissions that hurt everyone.

Our Mission

Direct Connection.
Zero Commission.

We don't take booking commissions. We don't add hidden fees. We simply connect travellers with verified hotels — and let them deal directly. It's better for travellers who get real prices, and better for hotels who keep every rupee they earn.

Our focus is on photo verification. Every hotel listed has submitted real photos that our team reviews before approving. No stock images, no misleading pictures — what you see is what you get.

How StayIndia Works

01
