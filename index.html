Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is StayIndia free to use?↓
Yes, completely free for visitors. Browse hotels, view photos, read reviews, and contact hotels directly at no charge — ever.
Do you take booking commission?↓
No. We are a directory, not a booking platform. You contact the hotel directly. There are no commissions, no middlemen, and no added fees.
How are hotels verified?↓
Every hotel listed has submitted real photos of their rooms, lobby, and facilities. Our team reviews and verifies before approving the listing.
Can I save favourite hotels?↓
Yes! Create a free account and save any hotel to your favourites. Your list is saved across devices so you can access it anytime.
How do I leave a review?↓
Sign in with your free account, visit any hotel detail page, and leave a star rating and written review. Reviews are moderated before publishing.
What cities are covered?↓
We currently cover 500+ cities across India, with a strong focus on Uttarakhand, Rajasthan, and Delhi NCR. We add new cities every week.
